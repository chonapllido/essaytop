Our Process

The Online Ordering Procedure and Delivery of Your Order

At first an idea of ordering a custom made essay or other paper online might seem rather risky. However, at MasterPapers.com you are absolutely secure. In this article you will be given all information necessary to know in order to form an impression of our online writing company.

What to Do First

Firstly, you need to browse our website to make sure we are what you’ve been looking for. The talented writers of MasterPapers produce high quality customized written assignments for students of all academic levels. The feedback of our clients is the best evidence of our expertise and qualifications of the writers.

How to Make an Order

When you are sure that we can accommodate your academic needs, you should proceed to the order page where all important information on your task has to be given. Once you have provided the details pertaining to your order and your preferred payment information, the verification message will be sent to your email.

The Discounts

If you order for the first time, you can make use of our special 20% off discount. Furthermore, later on you will benefit from a cumulative discount policy which increases your personal discount according to the volume of work you have ordered. The maximum will reach a 15% permanent discount.

The Writing Process

We work on the prepaid service basis. Therefore, when the payment has been made, our administration team assigns a writer to your task. You can be sure that only the most qualified person will be chosen specifically for your order. As you will be given access to our online messaging board, you will be able to communicate with your writer any time of the day, absolutely anonymously, by the way. In addition, you will be able to upload any information and documents which may be required for the completion of your order.

The Delivery

As soon as you paper is ready, you will be notified via an email about that. Then, either through direct download link or at your customer’s area on our website, you can download the finished piece.

Revisions and Guarantees

MasterPapers is a company dedicated to the high standards of quality. That is why when our clients happen to be dissatisfied with their orders, we offer free revisions during the first 2 weeks after the delivery. We promise to perfect your assignment until you are fully satisfied with our work. Regarding the authenticity you are also fully secure with MasterPapers. We run regular plagiarism checks on all our orders before delivering them to clients.

Our aim is to make the experience of ordering your custom papers online secure and smooth. If high quality writing service is what you need, then you have come to the right place!

Just follow these simple steps and get the best custom paper on the market

  • 1)  Step 1: Fill out the order form and provide your payment details
  • 2)  Step 2: Get a confirmation from our support team
  • 3)  Step 3: Supervise your order via our messaging system
  • 4)  Step 4: Download your custom paper from our site or have it emailed
